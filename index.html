Operations in the Presentation

  • → ↓ Click on the right half : Next Slide
  • ← ↑ Click on the left half : Previous Slide
  • 1 Shift+← : Show the First Slide
  • E Shift+→ : Show the Last Slide
  • R Return : Reload Current Slide ("Panic button" for WHITE screen)
  • F : Full Screen Mode
  • esc : Exit Full Screen Mode

How to Make Your Presentation

Short Story

Your presentation is based on the “contents.js” file. The texts and visual resources are described in it. Open the "index.htm" and click link!

How to Prepare the “contents.js”

Edit the “contents.js” file as you like. The “images” folder is referred from the “contents.js”, and you can put image files and other resources in it. The original “contents.js” file is a example of slides and functions.
